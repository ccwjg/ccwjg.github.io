Singapore (Image)

About:

Viewing this photoshopped image of "What Singapore Means to Me" is a visual representation of all that makes Singapore unique. The map outline of the island, orchid flower, laksa, durian and other local delicacies represent our rich culture and heritage. Transportation such as MRTs are testament to how far we have come in terms of infrastructure development while the presence of army personnel reflect our commitment towards national security. HDB flats symbolise home for many families here in Singapore while scenic views remind us why it's so special living here on this little red dot!
